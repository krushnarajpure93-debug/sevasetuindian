Frequently Asked Questions
Got questions? We have answers.
How do I cancel my booked ticket?
You can easily cancel your ticket by going to the 'My Bookings' section in your profile. Select the ticket and click 'Cancel'. Refunds are processed within 3-5 working days.
Are there any hidden charges?
No! At Seva Setu, transparency is our core value. The price you see at the checkout is the final price you pay. No hidden convenience fees.
Can I book a ticket for someone else?
Yes, absolutely! When filling out the passenger details form, simply enter the details of the person who will be traveling. The ticket will be sent to the email provided.
